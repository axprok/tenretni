Darren Floyd's Videographic Assignments (WM 2026)

Videographic Assignment 10: Supercut
Students will create a 1-2 videographic supercut. Using an many sources as you can collect, create a supercut around a specific, clearly identifiable object, line of dialogue, type of shot, or anything else you can think of to organize your project. Supercuts can be made by what is collected together, and also by what is excluded. This assignment must be exported as .mov or .mp4 and uploaded to the appropriate turn-in folder on Google Drive before class on the due date for full credit. Name your files using the following convention: VA1_wmusername_##.mp4 For example, VA1_ddfloyd_01.mp4
