Song of the Motherland (1936)

An old British recording of the famous patriotic Soviet song, recorded in 1942.
Also known as Wide is My Motherland (Широка страна моя родная).
Performer: Foden's Motor Works Band
Year Recorded: 1942
Composer: Isaak Dunayevsky (Исаак Дунаевский)
Lyricist: Based on Russian lyrics by Vasily Lebedev-Kumach (Василий Лебедев-Кумач)
