Fyodor Gladkov's novel Cement (1925) Machine idolatry

Chumalov: "the machines are all ready to run" (51-52)
"You are making an idol of the factory, Comrade Chumalov" (52)

Use of industrial terms, not known to average readers
ropeway/incline -- here in the novel a type of aerial lift system that uses cables to transport goods, especially in mountainous or urban areas

The Machine (the factory, the modern city) is a hero.
"The eyes of the factory opened, like electric moons" (148)
individual worker is martyred in the same chapter (149)
