Use of industrial terms, not known to average readers
бремсберг (bremsberg) corresponds to several established terms depending on context:
Inclined drift / incline → the general term for an inclined passage in a mine.
Self-acting incline (or gravity incline) → when loaded mine cars run down by gravity, controlled by a brake.
