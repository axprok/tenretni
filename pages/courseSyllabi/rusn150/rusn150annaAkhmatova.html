Anna Akhmatova

A poet. She became famous the 1900-1910s. During Stalin's rule wrote about hardship of life under a dictatorship and was censored.
Authored "Requiem" (1935-1940) a long poem about her experience in Leningrad during the Great Terror of 1937. Published in the USSR in 1987 during glasnost era.
Akhmatova was nominated twice for a Nobel Prize in literature (1956, 1966).
