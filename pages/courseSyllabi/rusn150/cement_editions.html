Fyodor Gladkov's novel Cement (1925). Different Editions

Gladkov published his novel in the early years after the revolution.
After Stalin took over the country, Gladkov revised his novel and
removed any graphic sexual scenes and
sections emphasizing gender agency.

For example, Love comradeship a la Kollontai: Dasha chooses to sleep with Badin (this last paragraph was removed from later (1940 and on) editions 134) published during Stalin rule. Love-making with Gleb after telling him about Badin, re-education of Gleb (139).
