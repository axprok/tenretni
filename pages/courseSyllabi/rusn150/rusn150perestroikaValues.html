Gorbachev's Perestroika and Gender (1985-1991)
New Values

    Values and Reforms:
  • glasnost (freedom of press, dismantling communist censorship)
  • perestroika (freedom in politics and economic activity)
  • Gender equity as human rights
  • Intersectionality (3rd wave): gender rights and struggle for national sovereignty.
    For example, in Lithuania women played a major role in the new government
    For example, Kazimira Prunskiene was a Lithuanian politician who was the first prime minister of Lithuania after the declaration of independence on 11 March 1990.
