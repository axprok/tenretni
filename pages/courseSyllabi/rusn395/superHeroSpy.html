Super Hero Spy Film

Black Widow (dir. Kate Shortland 2021) is a 24th film in the Marvel Cinematic Universe. It is a feminist action spy thriller fused with family melodrama. Note the representation of heroic masculinity as butt of all jokes: the Red Guardian (super soldier Aleksei Shostakov).

Natasha is trained in Red Room as a KGB assassin Black Widow and is recrutied later into the S.H.I.E.L.D. She is a founding member of the Avengers team.
