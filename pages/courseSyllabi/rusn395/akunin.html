Boris Akunin (1956-)

B. Akunin is a pen name of Grigorii Chkhartishvili. Born in Georgia; the most famous alum from my high school in Moscow; majored in Japanese culture at Moscow University; lived in Moscow until 2014; since then lives in Britain, France and Spain. On 24th of February 2022 Akunin wrote: "Russia is ruled by a psychologically deranged dictator and worst of all, it obediently follows his paranoia.”

Several cycles of novels and non-fiction . The Erast Fandorin cycle of crime and mystery novels is the best known one. The Winter Queen (Azazel)—1998 the first Erast Fandorin novel Turkish Gambit – 1998 the third of the Fandorin novel, the spy novel.

In 2024, Russia has designated Boris Akunin, one of the country's most popular novelists, a foreign agent for his criticism of Russia's aggression in Ukraine
