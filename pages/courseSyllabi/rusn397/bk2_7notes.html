The Brothers Karamazov.
Book 2, Chapter 7 notes
A Seminarist-Careerist


  • Mikhail Rakitin is a foil of Alesha (cynic vs. a sincere character)
    a modern type: an intellectual who will go either way: a career in the church (without faith) or
    a career as a fashionable journalist-socialist in St. Petersburg (82)
    tempts Alesha to start speaking sarcastically with venom instead of love and empathy
    Rakitin is the first imp-like character Alesha runs into after Zosima tells him to leave the monastery and be near both brothers (77)
    Rakitin prevents Alesha to be next to both borthers.
    Rakitin hates Zosima.
    Rakitin says that you can have a rule of law without God, without the immortality of soul (82).
