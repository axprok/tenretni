Boris Grigoryev's Illustations for Dostoevsky's Works

The only time the illustrations for The Brothers Karamazov appeared in public was in 1933, at an exhibition in New York. Because of the Great Depression in the U.S., a book with drawings by Grigoriev was never published. After that, they ended up in a private collection and didn’t appear in public. The second time , these illustrations were exhibitied at the retrospective exhibition of Boris Grigoriev at the Fabergé Museum in St. Petersburg (2024).
