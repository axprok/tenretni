Viktor Apukhtin's illustrations for Dostoevsky's The Idiot

Lev Myshkin
artist Viktor Apukhtin (2017)
pencil on paper. 21 x 29 cm
"The Idiot” was written on the theme of a line from the Gospel of
John (chapter 1, verse 10)
“He was in the world,
and the world was made by Him,
and the world knew Him not.”
