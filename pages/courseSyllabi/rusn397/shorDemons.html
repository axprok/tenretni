Sarra Shor's Illustations for Dostoevsky's Demons

Sarra Shor created illustrations for the Academia publishing house 1935 edition. At this time Lev Kamenev was in charge of the publishing house. On Stalin's orders, the print run was destroyed. Stalin's loyal critic David Zaslavsky called the edition initiative: "literary rot."
