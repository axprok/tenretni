Here is a discussion of Gail Lenhoff's theory of Sitz im Leben(vertical link of the text to the institutions that needed it).

Sitz im Leben is an approach common in Biblical criticism. It stands for the context in which a text, or object, has been created, and its function and purpose at that time. The Sitz im Leben is also used to refer to the social, ethnic and cultural setting of a site at a particular era.
