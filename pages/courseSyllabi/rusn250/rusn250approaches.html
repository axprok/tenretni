Approaches to Folklore and Folktales
  • Jakobson and Bogatyrev’s theory about folklore and its difference from literature (langue and parole, innovation, authorship)
  • thematic (animal, household, wonder tales) and motif approaches to the folk tale
  • Max Luthi’s stylistic approach and how to apply it to tales (Abstract Style, One-Dimensionality, Depthlessness)
  • Psychoanalysis. Bettelheim’s theory, how he categorizes different tales (and features of those types of tales) (“Youngest Child,” “Two Siblings”)
  • Structuralism
  • Feminist approaches to folk tales.
