The Tale of Peter and Fevronia of Murom
author: Hermolaus-Erasmus, a priest in one of Kremlin churces (1500s (16th century))

In Russia, the Day of Saints Peter and Fevronia (July 8) is celebrated as the Day of Love and Fidelity. They are considered as the patrons of marriage and family.
