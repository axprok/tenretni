New Year, Xmas, Yuletide

988-1700. Peasants. Yuletide together with Christmas
1700 Peter the Great: western style Xmas with the Xmas Tree. He saw this tradition in Germany.
1917 Communists: no religious (Christian and pagan) holidays: Xmas is cancelled, Yuletide is banned.
1935 Stalin: New Year celebration instead of Xmas and New Year Tree instead of Xmas tree.
