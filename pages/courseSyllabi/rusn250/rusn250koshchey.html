Koshchei the Deathless

  • -In Russian, he is Koshchei Bessmertnyi (Literally “Koshchei without death”)
  • -He is perpetually aged yet ageless. He is both immortal and mortal, old and young.
  • -He is skinny and bony with scraggly hair, and therefore suspicious.
  • -He is intimately connected with nature: his death is hidden away and protected by representatives of many realms of the natural world; he seems to have some power over natural forces.
  • -Traditionally, the folk tale motif usually associated with him is that the tale’s hero must find Koshchei’s death in order to defeat him.
  • -He can be a father figure.
  • -He can be outsmarted.
  • -He is a representative of the importance of secrecy.
