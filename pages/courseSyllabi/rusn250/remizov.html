Alexey Remizov (1877-1957)

Aleksey Remizov (1877-1957). Remizov is a modernist writer and artist who used hagiography to develop his own unique writer's style. He completed "About Pyotr and Fevronia of Murom" in 1951. It is based on the hagiographic tale "Peter and Fevronia" from the 1500s. Remizov was born in Moscow but after the communist revolution in October 1917, he moved to Paris.
