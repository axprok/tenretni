Primary Chronicle is compiled in Kyiv in 1037-1040.
Nestor is one of most famous scribes of the Primary Chronicle The Primary Chronicle follows the conventions of hagiography.
See for example lives of SS Boris and Gleb, the sons of St. Vladimir. They know that they will get killed but follow self-sacrificsing model life of JS.
Serge Zenkovsvky notes that Eastern Orthodox saints follow kenotic model of sainthood.
Greek Kenotic means self-emptying, non-resisting to violence.
