Tale of Tsar Saltan (1831)

The full title is "The Tale of Tsar Saltan, of His Son the Renowned and Mighty Bogatyr Prince Gvidon Saltanovich and of the Beautiful Swan-Princess." Pushkin's tale is based on the plot of a Russian folk take "Wicked Sisters". Pushkin's tale is written in trochaic tetrameter, a meter imitating the meter of the folk song. You can read more about Pushkin's uses of folklore in Michael Wachtel's article "Pushkin's Turn to Folklore."
