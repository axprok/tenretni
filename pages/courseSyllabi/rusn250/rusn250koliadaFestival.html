Koliada

    Koliada is a Slavic festival that takes place around Christmas and the Winter Solstice, and includes a variety of traditions:
  • Caroling: Groups of singers visit homes to sing special songs, called koliadky, to celebrate the holiday
  • Games and fortune telling: These activities are also part of the Koliada celebration
  • Bonfires: Bonfires are lit as part of the festivities
  • Dressing up in unusual clothes: People may dress up in unusual clothes or imitate animals to represent the dead souls who return to receive gifts from the living
