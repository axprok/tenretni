Skomorokh

Skomorokh was a traveling actor who composed oral/musical and dramatic performances, narrated folk tales.
They existed in Eastern Europe from 10th till 18th century.
They are mentioned in the Primary Chronicle.
Orthodox church condemned them as servants of the devil.

Russian filmmaker Andrei Tarkovskii depicts skomorokh performance in his film Andrei Rublev.
