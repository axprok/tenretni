Remizov. "About Peter and Favroniya of Murom" (1951)

  • Remizov retells "The Tale of Saints and Fevronia" (1500s).
  • the story is a modernist immitation of the style of hagiographic story and of the folk tale elements of this story.
  • They eere canonized in 1547 and are considered the patron saints of marriage.
  • In 2008, the Russian government declared their saints day a holiday of family love to counter the growing popularity of the Western celebration of St. Valentine's Day.
