Nikolai Rimskii-Korsakov. Opera Tale of Tsar Saltan

The opera is based on Alexander Pushkin's 1831 eponymous tale in verse. The opera was staged in Moscow to commemorate Pushkin's centenary. Russian artist Mikhail Vrubel Vrubel designed the decor and costumes for this opera. for the opera. His wife Nadezhda Zabela-Vrubel performed as Swan-Princess in this production.
