Boris Messerer's postcards depicting Skomorokhs (1972)

Boris Messerer has been making stage designs for plays in Moscow's best-known theaters and theaters in other Russian cities from 1960 till 1990s.

Messerer also worked a lot for TV in the USSR and Russia and made brilliant book illustrations. In 1980, together with his wife, outstanding poet Bella Akhmadulina, he took part in the publication of the well-known opposition magazine Metropol.
