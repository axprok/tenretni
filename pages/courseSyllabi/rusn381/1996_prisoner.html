Prisoner of the Mountains

  • trope of a Russian captive in the Caucasus
  • trope of a noble savage
  • Soviet Sasha vs. Russian Ivan
  • Sound. Use of music. Slavic Woman's Farewell.
    Famous Examples
  • James Fenimore Cooper’s novel The Last of the Mohicans (1826) – features captives and the noble savage trope.
  • John Ford’s film The Searchers (1956) – a man searching for his niece who has been taken by the Comanche.
  • Dances with Wolves (1990) – A revisionist take, where the protagonist assimilates into Lakota culture.
    • Avatar (2009) – Another revisionist film (or not so?), a sci-fi film, with a white man integrating into an alien society.
