Russian 393. Russia in Context. Syllabus.

Course Description and Objectives: RUSN 393: Russia in Context offers students a broad perspective on the locale of the summer 2023 Project Global Officer program – Vilnius, Lithuania. Through assigned readings, film screenings, and various excursions students will explore
  • the geography and brief history of the Baltic region and its connection to Russia
  • the intersection of cultures, religions, and languages that was the hallmark of pre-war Vilnius
  • the scars of World War II on the land and the peoples of this territory
  • the history and long-lasting effects of Soviet occupation
  • the various waves of migration of peoples in this region, including the most recent migrant crisis on the border between Belorusia and Lithuania
