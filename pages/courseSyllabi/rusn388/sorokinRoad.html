Day of the Oprichnik: the Road

In Day of the Oprichnik, the clairvoyant — Praskovia Mamontovna — lives near Orenburg. Andrei Komiaga travels there to consult her on behalf of the Tsarina. The journey to Orenburg also emphasizes the novel’s eastward orientation toward China and the Eurasian frontier in Vladimir Sorokin’s dystopian Russia.
