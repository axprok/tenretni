The Silver Bone

Setting: The novel takes place in 1919 Kyiv, amidst the chaos of the Ukrainian revolution and the harsh realities of the Soviet army's occupation.

Plot: The plot follows Samson Kolechko, a newly recruited investigator, as he tackles his first investigation involving a series of murders and a enigmatic stolen object.

Atmosphere: The story is described as a droll, surreal, and deeply atmospheric crime story that captures a dangerous, uncertain era of Ukrainian history
