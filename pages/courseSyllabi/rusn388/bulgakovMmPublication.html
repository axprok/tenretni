Master and Margarita. Publication Milestones

  • 1928–1940: Written in secrecy during the Stalinist era, with early drafts destroyed by Bulgakov in 1930.
  • 1966–1967: First publication, a censored serial version in the Moskva literary magazine (issues 11, 1966 and 1, 1967).
  • 1967: First foreign edition published in Italian by Einaudi
  • 1967: an Estonian translation by Eesti Raamat.
  • 1969: A version based on the Moskva text was published in Frankfurt.
  • 1973: First complete, uncensored Russian-language edition published by YMCA-Press in Paris.
