Rusn 388 Slang in One Day in the Life of Ivan Denisovich (1962).

  • Zek: camp inmate
  • Godfather (in Russian koom, кум): camp security officer
  • knocker (in Rusn: stookach, стукач): an informer working for the guards (p. 20)
  • BUR: penal cell within the camp (127)
  • Trusties (pridurok, shesterka): privileged prisoners (117)
  • Skilly (balanda): inmate's soup (127)
  • Old man whiskers: Stalin (130)
