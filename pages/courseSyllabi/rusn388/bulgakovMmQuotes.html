Master and Margarita. Famous Quotes

“Love leaped out at us from behind a corner, like a murderer in an alley, and struck us both at once!”
The speaker is Master.
The metaphor emphasizes the suddenness, violence, and inevitability of love in the novel. It is not gentle or gradual, but overwhelming.

Woland: “Never ask for anything, and especially from those who are stronger than you. They’ll make the offer themselves, and give everything themselves.” (282) Power gives; the weak ask.

“Manuscripts don’t burn.” (287)
In Russian:
«Рукописи не горят.»
Woland says it when he returns the Master’s destroyed novel to him.
Symbolically, it means: True art cannot be annihilated. Truth survives attempts at censorship.

Koroviev: “No papers, no person” (289)

Woland: "Your novel will still bring you surprises" (293).
