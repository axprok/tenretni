Master and Margarita. Famous Quotes

“Love leaped out at us from behind a corner, like a murderer in an alley, and struck us both at once!”
The speaker is Master.
The metaphor emphasizes the suddenness, violence, and inevitability of love in the novel. It is not gentle or gradual, but overwhelming.

“Manuscripts don’t burn.”
In Russian:
«Рукописи не горят.»
Woland says it when he returns the Master’s destroyed novel to him.
