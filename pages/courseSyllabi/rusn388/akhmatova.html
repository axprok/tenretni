Anna Akhmatova (1889-1966)

    What does it mean that she is an acmeist poet?
  • Acmeism = clarity, precision, concreteness
  • Short, compact, disciplined lyrics
  • Intimate “chamber” poetry
  • Love, loss, separation, unfulfilled desire
  • Emotion shown through tiny gestures
  • Concrete objects carry psychological weight
  • Female narrator with a strong sense of agency
  • Later poetry becomes civic but stays concrete
