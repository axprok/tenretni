Agnieszka Holland

Many of her most famous works confront painful historical events. For example:
Europa Europa – a widely acclaimed film about a Jewish boy surviving the Holocaust by posing as a Nazi. It won a Golden Globe and was nominated for an Academy Award for Best Adapted Screenplay.
In Darkness – about a Polish sewer worker hiding Jews during the Holocaust; it was nominated for the Academy Award for Best Foreign Language Film.
Mr. Jones – about journalist Gareth Jones exposing the Soviet famine in Ukraine.
The most controversial recent film by Agnieszka Holland is widely considered to be Green Border (2023)
