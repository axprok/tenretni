Ryanverse Main Films

  • The Hunt for Red October (1990): Alec Baldwin stars as Jack Ryan, analyzing a rogue Soviet captain (Sean Connery) with a new submarine.
  • Patriot Games (1992): Harrison Ford takes over as a former CIA analyst whose family is targeted by IRA terrorists.
  • Clear and Present Danger (1994): Harrison Ford returns as acting CIA Chief, navigating corruption while dealing with Colombian drug cartels.
  • The Sum of All Fears (2002): Ben Affleck portrays a younger Ryan trying to prevent a nuclear conflict in this reboot.
  • Jack Ryan: Shadow Recruit (2014): Chris Pine stars in an origin story where a young analyst uncovers a Russian plot to sabotage the U.S. economy
