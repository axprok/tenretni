Key Elements of a "Howcatchem" Plot

  • The Reveal: The audience knows the culprit and often sees the crime committed in the first chapter or act.
  • The Focus: The plot revolves around the investigative process, maneuvering, and intellectual battle between the antagonist and protagonist.
  • The Suspense: The tension comes from watching if/how the investigator will uncover evidence to prove the "seemingly impossible" crime.
  • Subplots: Often includes investigating why the crime was committed (subsidiary puzzles) or dealing with the criminal's efforts to disrupt the investigation.
