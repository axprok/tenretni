Rogue Tale and Europena Cinema: Europa Europa (1990)

A "rogue narrative" refers to a literary genre, popular from the 16th-17th centuries, featuring cunning, anti-heroic tricksters (picaros) surviving by their wits outside societal norms, often blending social commentary with adventure survival, identity, and often a satirical look at the world from the perspective of the marginalize. What does such a narrative mean when it is set in the 20th century?
